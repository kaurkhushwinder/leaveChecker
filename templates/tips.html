Pest Control

Organic Neem Shield

Mix 2 teaspoons of Neem oil with 1 teaspoon of mild soap in 1 liter of water. This kills aphids and mites without damaging the environment.

Irrigation

Morning Hydration

Water your crops before 8:00 AM. This prevents moisture from staying on leaves overnight, reducing fungal risk by 40%.

Soil Nutrition

Calcium Boost

Use crushed eggshells in the soil for Tomato and Pepper plants. It provides a slow-release calcium boost that prevents blossom end rot.

Fertilizer

Potassium Tea

Soak banana peels in water for 48 hours. This liquid is rich in potassium and helps in rapid fruit growth and healthy roots.
